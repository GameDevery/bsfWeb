Constructs a new texture buffer from a specific surface in the provided texture.

Parameters
[in]targetOpenGL type of the texture to retrieve the surface from.
[in]idOpenGL handle to the texture to retrieve the surface from.
[in]faceFace index of the texture in the case of cube textures or texture arrays.
[in]levelMip level of the texture.
[in]formatFormat of each pixel in the buffer.
[in]usageUsage signaling the render system how we plan on using the buffer.
[in]hwGammaTrue if buffer colors are assumed to be in sRGB space.
[in]multisampleCountNumber of samples the parent texture was created with.
