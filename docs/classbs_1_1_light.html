)
static

Creates a new light with provided settings.

Parameters
[in]typeType of light that determines how are the rest of the parameters interpreted.
[in]colorColor of the light.
[in]intensityPower of the light source. This will be luminous flux for radial & spot lights, luminance for directional lights with no area, and illuminance for directional lights with area (non-zero source radius).
[in]attRadiusRadius at which light's influence fades out to zero.
[in]castsShadowsDetermines whether the light casts shadows.
[in]spotAngleTotal angle covered by a spot light.
[in]spotFalloffAngleSpot light angle at which falloff starts. Must be smaller than total angle.
