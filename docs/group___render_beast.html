Enumeration Type Documentation

◆ RenderBeastFiltering

enum RenderBeastFiltering
strong

Texture filtering options for RenderBeast.

Enumerator
Bilinear 

Sample linearly in X and Y directions within a texture mip level.

Trilinear 

Sample bilinearly and also between texture mip levels to hide the mip transitions.

Anisotropic 

High quality dynamic filtering that improves quality of angled surfaces.
