◆ calcRoughnessFadeScaleBias()

static Vector2 calcRoughnessFadeScaleBias ( float  maxRoughness)
static

Calculates a scale & bias that is used for transforming roughness into a fade out value.

Anything that is below maxRoughness will have the fade value of 1. Values above maxRoughness is slowly fade out over a range that is 1/2 the length of maxRoughness.
