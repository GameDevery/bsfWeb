Creates a descriptor for a two dimensional render texture.

Parameters
[in]formatPixel format used by the texture surface.
[in]widthWidth of the render texture, in pixels.
[in]heightHeight of the render texture, in pixels.
[in]usageUsage flags that control in which way is the texture going to be used.
[in]samplesIf higher than 1, texture containing multiple samples per pixel is created.
[in]hwGammaShould the written pixels be gamma corrected.
[in]arraySizeNumber of textures in a texture array. Specify 1 for no array.
[in]mipCountNumber of mip levels, excluding the root mip level.
Returns
Descriptor that is accepted by RenderTexturePool.
